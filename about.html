Introduction

A South African
fuel solution
company.

Tingi Financial Services (Pty) Ltd, trading as Tingi Petroleum Services, is a South African-based fuel solutions company headquartered in Midrand, Johannesburg. The company was founded to address inefficiencies and lack of centralisation in the diesel refueling market.

With over six years of industry experience, Tingi Petroleum provides reliable, affordable, and sustainable fuel solutions across South Africa and the broader African continent. We supply more than just fuel — we deliver reliability, efficiency, and sustainability across the energy value chain.

From bulk diesel supply through to mobile refueling, tank installation and on-site product testing — every operation is built on a single principle: get the right fuel to the right place, at the right time, every time.
